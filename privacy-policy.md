# Privacy Policy for StoryChan
Last updated: November 2025  
Developer: SRAI Labs  
Contact: rajmemo896@gmail.com  

StoryChan ("the App") is developed by SRAI Labs.  
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect user information.

---

## 1. Information We Collect
### a) Google Account Information  
When you sign in using Google Sign-In, we collect:
- Name
- Email address
- Profile photo
- Google UID (unique user ID)

This data is automatically stored securely in **Firebase Authentication** and **Firestore**.

### b) App Usage & Story Data  
We store:
- Rooms you create  
- Joined rooms  
- Story text you write  
- Players in the room  
- Timestamps & turn order  

All stored in Firebase Firestore.

### c) Device Information (Non-Personal)
We may automatically collect:
- Device model  
- OS version  
- Crash logs  
Used only for debugging & app improvement.

---

## 2. How Your Information Is Used
We use your data **only** for:
- Authenticating you in the app  
- Syncing rooms and stories  
- Allowing collaboration with friends  
- Managing turns & room permissions  
- Improving app performance  

We **do not**:
- Sell data  
- Share data with third parties  
- Use data for advertising  

---

## 3. Data Storage & Security
Data is stored in:
- Firebase Authentication  
- Firebase Firestore  

Firebase provides:
- Encrypted storage  
- Secure Google-managed servers  
- Authentication-required access  

Only authenticated users may read/write their own data.
