# Story Chan - Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Story Chan ("the App") is developed by SRAI LABS ("we", "our", "us").
This Privacy Policy explains how user data is handled within the App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect
### a) Google Account Information  
When you sign in using Google Sign-In, we collect:
- Name
- Email address
- Profile photo
- Google UID (unique user ID)

This data is automatically stored securely in **Firebase Authentication** and **Firestore**.

### b) App Usage & Story Data  
We store:
- Rooms you create  
- Joined rooms  
- Story text you write  
- Players in the room  
- Timestamps & turn order  

All stored in Firebase Firestore.

### c) Device Information (Non-Personal)
We may automatically collect:
- Device model  
- OS version  
- Crash logs  
Used only for debugging & app improvement.

---

## 2. How Your Information Is Used
We use your data **only** for:
- Authenticating you in the app  
- Syncing rooms and stories  
- Allowing collaboration with friends  
- Managing turns & room permissions  
- Improving app performance  

We **do not**:
- Sell data  
- Share data with third parties  
- Use data for advertising  

---

## 3. Data Storage & Security
Data is stored in:
- Firebase Authentication  
- Firebase Firestore  

Firebase provides:
- Encrypted storage  
- Secure Google-managed servers  
- Authentication-required access  

Only authenticated users may read/write their own data.

---

## 4. Data Deletion
You may delete your data at any time.  
Instructions:  
https://deadsaw.github.io/storychan-privacy/data-deletion

### **a. Google Sign-In Data**
When you sign in using Google, we receive:
- Your Google account ID
- Your name
- Your email address
- Your profile picture (if available)

We do *not* collect passwords.

### **b. Story Rooms & User Content**
We store:
- Room names
- Room codes
- Player lists
- Story text written collaboratively

This data is stored in **Firebase Firestore**.

### **c. Usage Data**
We may collect anonymous analytics such as:
- Number of rooms created
- App crash logs
- Device info (Android version)

No personally identifiable information is used for analytics.

---

## 2. How We Use the Data

We use collected data to:
- Authenticate users
- Create and join story rooms
- Sync stories in real-time
- Improve the app and fix bugs

We **never sell or share** your data with third parties.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services We Use

The app uses:
- **Firebase Authentication**
- **Firebase Firestore**
- **Google Play Services**

These services may collect information as per their own privacy policies.

## 4. Data Retention

- User accounts remain in Firebase until deleted.
- Room data and stories remain until removed by users.
- We do not retain user data outside Firebase. Anonymous analytics data may be processed by Firebase Analytics.
